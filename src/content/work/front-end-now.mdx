## What Front End Now is

I co-founded **Front End Now** to solve the real bottleneck beginners hit: not motivation — **direction**. Most people don’t fail because they’re incapable; they fail because they optimize the wrong things too early.

## What I actually do

- **Tight feedback loops** — daily support beats passive content consumption.
- **Translate hiring reality** — what teams evaluate in portfolios, interviews, and take-home work.
- **Keep standards modern** — the frontend toolchain moves; curriculum has to move with it without chasing novelty.

## Proof that matters

Students have landed **70+ job offers** (aggregate outcome — individual timelines vary). The metric I care about most is whether someone can **ship credible UI** and explain trade-offs like an engineer.

## Philosophy (short)

Coding is boring and hard at the beginning — that’s normal. My job is to keep people from quitting in the worst possible window: right after it gets confusing.
