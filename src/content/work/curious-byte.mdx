## Why this is here

Not every piece of work is proprietary. Teaching forces you to ship clarity — sometimes that clarity becomes a **portfolio-grade reference build**.

## What the project demonstrates

- **Next.js 13** patterns students can extend without getting lost in novelty.
- **Strapi** as a pragmatic CMS boundary for content-driven apps.
- **MUI + TypeScript** for interfaces that look intentional even when speed matters.

## Teaching lens

The goal isn’t “clone a SaaS”. It’s help someone understand **how pieces connect**: routing, data fetching boundaries, auth-ish flows, and UI composition — in an order that mirrors real adoption.
